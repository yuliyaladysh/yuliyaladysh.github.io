About

About me

Hello! I am Yuliya Ladysh — a passionate UX/UI designer with a knack for creating captivating and intuitive digital journeys. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of user psychology, I craft visually stunning interfaces that seamlessly blend form and function. From wireframes to pixel-perfect prototypes, I thrive on transforming complex concepts into delightful user experiences. Let’s collaborate and turn your vision into a breathtaking reality that will leave your users hooked from the very first click.

Typical engagements span discovery workshops through UI specs — always anchored in real user needs.

  • UX & research

  • UI & systems

  • Web & product

    Marketing sites, onboarding, paywalls, and dense desktop flows — layout, hierarchy, and microcopy.
